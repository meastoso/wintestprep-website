Mrs. Roberta Wolman Papas

Director

Director, Roberta Wolman Papas, has been preparing students for college entrance exams throughout New England since 1975. Ms. Wolman is an educational tutor a study skills expert and a reading and learning specialist for students in grades 7-12. Recently, Ms. Wolman was a regional admissions associate for John Cabot University of Rome, Italy. Previously, Ms. Wolman offered a Study Skills summer programs for elite high school students at Boston University.

While in Lexington, MA, she was an instrumental leader in building a Language Arts program at Minuteman High School. This included integrating study skills and reading across the curriculum. Her enthusiastic knack for inspiring reluctant readers led to the development of a school wide summer reading and her role as Task Force Chairperson. Ms. Wolman possesses a thorough background in educational assessment and a particular expertise in assessing language development and cognition.

As an educational consultant Ms. Wolman presented professional development workshops throughout New England, training professionals in the incorporation of study skills and advanced reading skills.

Ms. Wolman earned an MS. Ed at Lesley College and was a Doctoral candidate at Boston University. She holds Massachusetts certification in Reading, Reading Supervison K-12, Elementary Education, English 6 -12, Principal and Special Education. Ms. Wolman's diverse background offers a rich experience to students and their families in the private tutorial learning environment.
