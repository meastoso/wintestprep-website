About WinTestPrep

Wintestprep offers private individualized instruction. The instructor Roberta Wolman, is formerly a full time Secondary English Teacher as well as a Reading and Learning disabilities specialist in Lexington, MA. Additionally, she has a specialized focus on Study Skills. Ms. Wolman works with students and their families who are seeking a tailored one on one instructional relationship.

The curriculum is based on each individual's learning style, and tutorial sessions are scheduled according to practical necessities such as extra curricular commitments and nonschool related activities. The digitized curriculum, based on best practices, is continuously revised and updated and reflects Ms. Wolman's vast and varied experiences over many decades in the classroom and involvement in college admissions and recruitment and in the private educational sector.
